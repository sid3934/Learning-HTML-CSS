Rohit's memorable innings at Perth

In the first ODI of the 2016 India tour of Australia, Rohit scored an unbetean record breaking 171 runs. The significance of this innings is such that it is the second highest individual score by a visiting batsman in Australia the highest being Jason Roy's 180. Rohit tops the list of highest individual score by Indians wth a distant second being the legendary Saurav Ganguly's 141 back in 2000.
