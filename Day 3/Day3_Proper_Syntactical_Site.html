The right heading.

The body that is rendered on the browser should have the right syntax. Proper syntax means: 1. Head, 2. Body. 2 breaks down has 2.1 header, 2.2 Main 2.3 Footer. 2.1 may contain a navigation.
2.2 breaks down as 2.2.1 section, 2.2.2 article and if needed 2.2.3 aside.
Having a proper syntax for a webpage boosts user experience and also enhances the page's visibility for a search engine.

The aside tag gives flexibility of displaying not so imp content.
